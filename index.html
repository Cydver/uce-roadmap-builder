How to edit

  • Click catalog item Add, then drag the icon card.
  • Click month headers to rename them; right-click month headers to add left/right, delete, or switch between 4 and 5 weeks.
  • Drag colored bars to move meta duration. Drag handles to resize.
  • Select a unit, then left-click or right-click an empty chart cell to add a segment at that week.
  • Use Row position, or drag near a row boundary, to place a unit in-row or visually between adjacent pull rows.
  • Right-click units/bars for quick actions; double-click an MS or pilot to open the Selected unit editor window.
  • Click a legend status to edit its label/color.
  • Click left row headers to rename or recolor them.
